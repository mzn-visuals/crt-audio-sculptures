# Changelog

## [1.2] - 2026-06-22

### Changed
- "Now playing" pill at the top of the GUI now displays the song's name instead of "playing file"
- Redesigned music controls and merged them with the now playing bar under the search bar:
  - Play/pause combined into a single button
  - Added previous/next track buttons
  - Added a volume button that mutes/unmutes on click and reveals a volume slider on hover; scrolling the slider adjusts volume in 10% increments
  - Added a seekbar showing the current playback position and the song's total duration

## [1.1] - 2026-06-21

### Fixed
- Parsing issue that occurred on the first search (no longer requires reloading the page)
